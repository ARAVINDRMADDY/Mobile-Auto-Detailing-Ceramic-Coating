The Ultimate Shield:
Ceramic Coating

Wax is a thing of the past. Our professional-grade ceramic coatings utilize liquid nano-technology to bond with your vehicle's clear coat on a molecular level. The result is a semi-permanent glass-like shield.

  • Extreme Hydrophobicity

    Water beads up and rolls off immediately, taking dirt and grime with it. Washing your car becomes effortless.

  • UV & Oxidation Protection

    Blocks harmful ultraviolet rays preventing your paint from fading, oxidizing, or looking dull over time.

  • Unmatched Candy Gloss

    Enhances the reflective properties of your paint, leaving a deep, wet-looking gloss that lasts for years.
