Behind the Polish

The ProDetail Process

01

Decontamination & Prep

Every perfect finish starts with a perfectly clean slate. We begin with a pH-neutral snow foam to lift loose dirt, followed by a chemical iron remover and a mechanical clay bar treatment to pull embedded contaminants out of the clear coat.

02

Multi-Stage Correction

Using dual-action polishers and professional-grade compounds, we safely level the clear coat. This process permanently removes swirl marks, wash scratches, and water spots, restoring the absolute maximum gloss and clarity to your paint.

03

Ceramic Preservation

Once the paint is flawless, we lock in the result. Our hand-applied nano-ceramic coatings bond molecularly with the clear coat, providing years of extreme hydrophobic protection against UV rays, bird droppings, and environmental fallout.
