Our Story

How The Shine Started.

Founded over a decade ago, ProDetail was born from a simple realization: automatic car washes and quick drive-throughs were damaging paint and leaving true automotive enthusiasts frustrated.

What started as a small two-man mobile operation has now grown into the premier mobile detailing and ceramic coating service. We wanted to bridge the gap between high-end shop quality and the convenience of coming straight to your driveway.

Today, we've restored thousands of vehicles, empowering owners to drive with pride while preserving the long-term value of their investment.
